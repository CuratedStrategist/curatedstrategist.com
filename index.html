The Story Behind the Strategy

Built from the inside out.

I didn't stumble into growth strategy. I earned it over two decades inside Fortune 500 enterprises and high-growth organizations — building what didn't exist yet.

Enterprise Sales Systems AI & Automation Strategy Business Development Multi-State Operations Talent Acquisition

Over 20 years across large-scale enterprises, I was the person organizations called when they needed something built that had never been built before. I helped launch experimental inbound sales channels that scaled from small pilot teams to over 300 reps across two shifts — becoming some of the most productive sales departments in their organizations.

When rapid growth created a dangerous gap between classroom training and live sales performance, I designed and launched a hands-on sales coaching academy that dramatically reduced new hire churn, saved significant revenue in turnover costs, and became a permanent fixture in the organization.

In the financial services space, I was brought in by a multi-state mortgage company as a business development consultant — scouting and hiring brokers across markets while simultaneously building and running their virtual bot assistant to streamline operations and client engagement. Delivering under a $500K performance contract gave me a deep appreciation for how AI, when implemented thoughtfully, can transform how a business scales.

"I know what it feels like to be inside a legacy organization that has to reinvent itself — under pressure, at scale, with real stakes."

Today I bring that same builder's mindset to service-based businesses and enterprises of all kinds. Not someone who can demo a tool. Someone who can transform a team, architect a system, and bring an entire organization along for the journey.
